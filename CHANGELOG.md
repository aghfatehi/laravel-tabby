# Changelog

## v1.0.0 (2024-05-04)

- Initial release
- Full Tabby Checkout flow (Create Session, Callback, Cancel, Failure)
- Capture and Refund payments
- Webhook management (handling payment_authorized, payment_captured, payment_failed, payment_rejected)
- Payment details retrieval
- Multi-region support (SA, AE, KW)
- Multi-currency support (SAR, AED, KWD)
- Sandbox & Production environments
- Configurable routes and middleware
- Transaction logging migration
- Laravel 10, 11, 12 & 13 support
- PHP 8.1+ support
